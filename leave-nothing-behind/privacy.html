Leave Nothing Behind

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 18, 2026

Short version: Leave Nothing Behind does not collect, store, or share any personal information — ever.

No Data Collection

Leave Nothing Behind does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, diagnostics, or any other information from users. No data of any kind leaves your device.

No Account Required

The app requires no account creation, sign-in, or registration of any kind. You can use every feature of Leave Nothing Behind without providing any personal details.

No Third-Party Services

Leave Nothing Behind does not integrate with any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers. There are no SDKs that track your behaviour or device.

Local Storage Only

Any information you enter or generate within the app is stored exclusively on your device. It is never transmitted to any server or external service.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data whatsoever, Leave Nothing Behind is safe for users of all ages and fully complies with COPPA and similar children's privacy regulations worldwide.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Given our commitment to zero data collection, we do not anticipate material changes.
